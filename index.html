ABOUT

Birmingham, AL based web developer that is constantly working to expand my programming knowledge and prowess. Always looking for new professional opportunities and chances to collaborate on new & exciting projects. Proud alumnus of Auburn University and the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB). Initially, I earned an education & expertise in the healthcare industry which taught me great analytical skills and client, customer, & interpersonal relationship building abilities. After gaining further exposure to the world of development and information systems through friends and family, it sparked a passion within me which has lead to a career transition into the information technology industry. Subsequently, I obtained my formal certification as a full-stack web developer and have continued to design and develop state-of-the-art, responsive, & user-friendly websites and web applications. To find out more about me and my work continue reading below.
